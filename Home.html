Chance the Rapper

Chance the Rapper is a twenty year old rapper from Chicago, Illinois. Following a ten day suspension in Spring 2011 during his Senior Year, Chance released his first mixtape titled 10 day. In April 2013, he released his second mixtape, Acid Rap. Check him out at Showbox SoDo on December 15th! Doors open at 8.

Husky Basketball

Join Lorenzo Romar and the Men's University of Washington Basketball Team as they play against the Idaho State Bengals on December 14th 1:00 PM at Alaska Airlines Arena. The Washington State defense is currently ranked 342nd in the nation, but Idaho has lost two of their past four games. It should be a close one!

Girls Day!

At the Computer Clubhouse of Tacoma on December 14th girls will be able to discover their own voice while engaging in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) activities. They will learn web design, robotics, video production, mobile app development, game development, and much more! Starts at 10:30 AM!
